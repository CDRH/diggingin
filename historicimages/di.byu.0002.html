Elk Horn River Ferry

Photographer/Artist/Creator:  Frederick Hawkins Piercy, 1830–1891
1855

Steel engraving, b&w, 16.5 x 25.5 cm.

Steel engraving of the Elk Horn River Ferry at the Elkhorn River Crossing, also known as the Elkhorn Crossing Site. Engraving also shows wagons and campsites. Taken from Route from Liverpool To Great Salt Lake Valley / illustrated with steel engravings and wood cuts from sketches made by Frederick Piercy, edited by James Linforth, opposite p. 55.
